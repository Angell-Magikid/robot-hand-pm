🎯项目定位与原则

"生物实验室的灵巧手"聚焦实验流程中高频、重复、对精度要求高的关键操作环节，通过灵巧手、机械臂与自动化控制系统的结合，提升实验室操作效率和标准化水平。

第一期优先原则：

  • ✓ 聚焦可落地、可验证、可收费的应用场景
  • ✓ 优先完成真实场景的样机开发与功能验证
  • ✓ 先形成收入闭环，再逐步拓展技术方向

🔬第一期案例与技术方向

核心切入点：实验室移液、抓取、挤压、转移自动化

技术重点：

  • 实验室线下调研与需求确认
  • 末端执行器设计与稳定性验证
  • 精度与重复执行一致性控制
  • 原型快速迭代（1-2个月内可用）

🎯Project Vision & Principles

The "Bio-Lab Dexterous Robot Hand" focuses on high-frequency, repetitive operations requiring precision in lab workflows. By combining dexterous grippers, robotic arms, and automated control systems, we enhance lab efficiency and standardization.

Phase 1 Priorities:

  • ✓ Focus on deliverable, verifiable, and billable scenarios
  • ✓ Prioritize prototype development and function verification
  • ✓ Establish revenue loop first, then expand technology

🔬Phase 1 Cases & Tech Direction

Core Focus: Lab pipetting, grasping, squeezing, and transfer automation

Technical Focus:

  • On-site lab research and requirement confirmation
  • End-effector design and stability verification
  • Precision and repeatability control
  • Rapid prototype iteration (usable in 1-2 months)
