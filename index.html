🎯 项目定位与原则 Project Vision & Principles

"生物实验室的灵巧手"聚焦实验流程中高频、重复、对精度要求高的关键操作环节，通过灵巧手、机械臂与自动化控制系统的结合，提升实验室操作效率和标准化水平。

The "Bio-Lab Dexterous Robot Hand" focuses on high-frequency, repetitive operations requiring precision in lab workflows. By combining dexterous grippers, robotic arms, and automated control systems, we enhance lab efficiency and standardization.
